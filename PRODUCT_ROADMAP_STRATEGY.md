## 1. Advanced Analytics Features to Add

### 1.1 Voice & Emotion Analytics

| Feature | Description | Business Value |
|---------|-------------|----------------|
| **Tone Analysis** | Detect voice tone (friendly, frustrated, confused, confident) | Identify at-risk customers, coach agents on tone |
| **Pitch Detection** | Analyze pitch variations indicating stress or excitement | Spot emotional peaks in conversations |
| **Speech Rate** | Words per minute analysis | Identify rushed or slow explanations |
| **Silence Detection** | Long pauses and dead air analysis | Find confusion points, measure think time |
| **Interruption Tracking** | Who interrupts whom and how often | Coach on active listening |
| **Talk-to-Listen Ratio** | Percentage of time agent vs customer speaks | Ideal is 40-60% agent talk time |
| **Energy Level** | Voice energy throughout the call | Detect enthusiasm or fatigue |

### 1.2 Conversation Intelligence Metrics

| Metric | What It Measures | Why It Matters |
|--------|------------------|----------------|
| **First Call Resolution (FCR)** | Issue resolved in single call | Higher FCR = lower costs, happier patients |
| **Average Handle Time (AHT)** | Total call duration | Efficiency benchmark |
| **Hold Time Analysis** | Time patient spent on hold | Patient frustration indicator |
| **Script Adherence Score** | How closely agent followed guidelines | Compliance & consistency |
| **Question Density** | Questions asked by agent | Discovery & engagement measure |
| **Objection Count** | Number of concerns raised | Sales resistance indicator |
| **Commitment Indicators** | Verbal cues of buy-in ("I'll try that", "sounds good") | Conversion likelihood |
| **Next Steps Clarity** | Were follow-ups clearly defined? | Patient compliance predictor |

### 1.3 Healthcare-Specific Metrics

| Metric | Description | Application |
|--------|-------------|-------------|
| **Symptom Capture Score** | How thoroughly symptoms were documented | Clinical quality |
| **Treatment Adherence Signals** | Patient commitment to follow treatment | Outcome prediction |
| **Pain Scale Mentions** | References to pain levels (1-10 scale) | Track patient progress |
| **Medication Compliance** | Discussion about taking medications | Patient safety |
| **Follow-up Scheduling** | Was next appointment scheduled? | Patient retention |
| **Referral Mentions** | Specialist referrals discussed | Care coordination |
| **Insurance/Billing Clarity** | Were costs clearly explained? | Reduce billing disputes |
| **Patient Education Score** | How well conditions were explained | Health literacy |
